Egypt's Landmarks

The great pyramids of Giza are the last standing wonder of the ancient world, the Pyramids defy time—monumental tombs of pharaohs, built with precision that still baffles modern engineers.
The temples of Karnak are a sprawling stone forest of towering columns, obelisks, and sanctuaries, Karnak was the beating heart of worship, power, and mythology for over 2,000 years.
The valley of kings is Hidden in the desert cliffs of Luxor, this necropolis cradled Egypt’s greatest rulers, its tombs whispering secrets of gold, curses, and the quest for immortality.
